Our Studio

Eon Arc is an international architecture practice focused on creating timeless, minimal spaces where light, matter, and proportion define a quiet sense of permanence.

We collaborate with clients to deliver sustainable, functional, and beautiful architecture, exploring every scale from furniture to city planning. Our approach is research-based, design-led, and deeply attentive to materiality, light, and form.
